About Stock Research Hub

Welcome to Stock Research Hub, your all-in-one platform for stock market and mutual fund analysis. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting, our platform provides real-time financial data, performance tracking, and interactive tools to help you make informed investment decisions.

🔍 Key Features

  • ✅ Stock & Mutual Fund Research – Get in-depth financial insights, NAV history, and CAGR analysis.
  • ✅ Mutual Fund Comparison – Easily compare multiple mutual funds based on key metrics and performance trends.
  • ✅ Interactive Data Visualization – Analyze trends with dynamic charts powered by Highcharts for a better understanding of market movements.
  • ✅ Real-Time API Integration – Fetch live stock and mutual fund data directly from trusted sources.
  • ✅ Smart Search & Suggestions – Use auto-suggest features to quickly find mutual funds and stocks of interest.
  • ✅ User-Friendly Interface – Our intuitive design ensures seamless navigation across different sections.
